About Restaurant

Check our story

Welcome to our Region Punjab, the most authentic Punjabi restaurant in town! We take pride in serving you the most delectable dishes from the vibrant state of Punjab, India. Our menu is carefully curated to bring you a tantalizing culinary experience that will transport your taste buds to the land of bhangra and warm hospitality. At Region Punjab, we offer a variety of Punjabi specialties that will truly satisfy your cravings. From our mouthwatering tandoori dishes prepared in the traditional clay oven to our rich and creamy curries, every bite is a burst of aromatic spices and flavors. Whether you are a vegetarian or a meat lover, our menu has something to suit every palate. Sample our succulent butter chicken, tender mutton kebabs, or indulge in our famous Punjabi thali that features a medley of dishes showcasing the diverse flavors of Punjab. What sets Region Punjab apart is our dedication to using only the freshest and finest ingredients. We source our produce locally whether it's succulent vegetables, aromatic spices, or tender meat, Region Punjab takes immense pride in its farm-to-table approach. The emphasis on using organic and natural ingredients ensures that the food is free from harmful chemicals and artificial food colors. The vibrant colors and tantalizing flavors of Punjabi cuisine come from the essence of freshly prepared spices and herbs. With traditional cooking methods and recipes passed down through generations, each dish is prepared with love and care. So, when you savor the delectable flavors of Punjab, you can be assured of a culinary experience that is both nourishing and wholesome experience.
